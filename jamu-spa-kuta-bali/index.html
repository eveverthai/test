Jamu Spa at Kuta – The Fine Art of Relaxation

JAMU Traditional Spa at AlamKulKul Boutique Resorts Bali is set within a beautiful facility with the Balinese Village theme of the resort. At Jamu Spas, we offer authentic health and beauty treatments from the islands of Bali and Java, using only the finest ingredients freshly-prepared according to the age-old tradition of Jamu.

Jamu Traditional Spa offer a variety of treatments to help you relax, relieve stress and revitalize yourself.

Jamu Traditional Spa prepares all of its own spa products following age-old recipes to ensure a level of quality that is consistent, pure and natural. Local plants, tropical flowers, fresh roots and sea-salt crystals are blended by hand to create a range of fragrant and gentle body products. All our products are100% vegan and are entirely inspired by nature.
